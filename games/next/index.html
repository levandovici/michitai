Game Design

Genre: 3D open-sea adventure (focus on action/exploration).

Core Idea: Control a 3D stylized pirate ship in real-time, sail vast oceans, fish dynamic sea creatures (e.g., Emperor Angelfish flocks via Simple Boids), and hunt treasures on islands (Mini Pirates Island, Island Assets). Upgrade ship/crew (3D Pirates Pack, Low Poly Animals) with loot.

Key Mechanics:

  1. Boat Control: 3D physics-based sailing (Unity's Rigidbody/colliders). Use WASD/arrows for movement, mouse for steering/camera. Wind affects speed; ram enemy ships or avoid obstacles.
  2. Fishing: From boat deck—cast lines/nets at fish schools. Real-time reeling with button inputs; catch rares for rewards.
  3. Treasure Hunt: Sail to islands, dock, explore on foot (third-person view) to dig up chests. Combat rivals with cannons/swords.
